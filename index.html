About: This website is designed to help people understand the imperialism that the United States had pursued during the 20th century. It includes various details on the definition of and reasons for imperialism, in addition with a timeline from 1900 to 1999 that depicts the historical imperialism events that had happened during the time period. Furthermore, at the end of the timeline is an interactive trivia game that helps users better understand how the United States imperialized other countries. By interacting with the timeline and the game, we hope that people would be more educated on these historical events, learning about not just concepts of imperialism, but also the whole history behind it.
